Scope

This policy covers the public DELTA Plant website at deltaplant.ai, including the static landing page, legal pages, and the contact request flow.

Core principle

We keep the public site lightweight: no sign-up wall, no advertising stack, and no hidden user profiling intentionally deployed by DELTA Plant.

Contact data

When you send a message through the contact form, the submitted data is processed only to handle your request and related follow-up.

Section 1

Overview

DELTA Plant is a public-facing project website presenting AI plant-health workflows, research assets, benchmark material, and access points such as DELTAPLANO. The website is delivered as a static experience and only processes personal data where technically necessary or where visitors explicitly decide to contact the project.

Controller contact. Privacy requests related to this website can be sent through the contact form on the homepage or through the public project channels linked in the footer.
Section 2

Data we process

The website may involve the following categories of data:

  • Technical access data: IP address, browser metadata, device information, timestamps, referrer information, and request logs that may be handled by the hosting and delivery infrastructure.
  • Contact request data: name, email address, organization, subject, message content, and any information voluntarily provided through the contact form.
  • Interaction data with external services: if you open Telegram, GitHub, or other external links, the destination service may process its own request and account-related data under its own policies.
  • Media delivery data: if you enable soundtrack playback or load external media resources, the corresponding provider may receive technical delivery information needed to stream that asset.
No account creation on site. DELTA Plant does not currently require a website account, login, newsletter subscription, or marketing automation flow for standard browsing.
Section 3

Purposes and legal bases

Where applicable, data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Website delivery and security: to serve the static pages, protect the infrastructure, troubleshoot faults, and keep the site available. This generally relies on legitimate operational interest and technical necessity.
  • Handling contact requests: to review submitted inquiries, respond to partnership, research, support, or collaboration requests, and maintain the conversation if follow-up is needed. This relies on visitor consent and request handling necessity.
  • Compliance and record-keeping: where needed to document legitimate operational activity, security incidents, or abuse prevention.

DELTA Plant does not intentionally use the public website to sell personal data or to build advertising profiles about visitors.

Section 5

Retention and security

Contact request data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to review, reply to, and document the relevant request. Technical infrastructure data may be retained by hosting or relay providers according to their operational retention schedules.

DELTA Plant uses a static-public-site model to reduce the privacy surface. Even so, no internet-facing service can guarantee absolute security, so visitors should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive data through the website form.

Section 6

Your rights

Depending on the laws applicable to your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability in relation to personal data processed through the website.

  • Use the DELTA Plant contact form to submit a privacy-related request.
  • Include enough information for the request to be matched to the relevant contact message or interaction.
  • If a third-party provider controls the relevant data directly, you may also need to contact that provider under its own privacy program.
Section 7

Updates and contact

This policy may be updated when the site structure, delivery providers, or contact workflow changes. The most recent revision date is shown at the top of this page.
