Cookie Policy

This page describes how the DELTA Plant website uses cookies or similar identifiers, and how third-party delivery resources may behave when visitors load optional features or submit the contact form.

Last updated: 15 May 2026 Static website with minimal tracking surface No advertising cookies intentionally deployed

No first-party marketing stack

DELTA Plant does not intentionally deploy first-party advertising, retargeting, or behavioral analytics cookies on the public landing page.

Optional third-party interactions

Third-party services are involved only for delivery of fonts, styling, audio, hosting, repository links, and form relay.

Browser control remains available

Visitors can block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings and can avoid optional features such as soundtrack playback or form submission.

Section 1

Overview

A cookie is a small text file stored by a browser. Similar technologies can include local storage, cached identifiers, or provider-managed session tokens. This policy explains the current website behavior of deltaplant.ai and the external services used to deliver the experience.

Section 2

Current cookie posture

At the time of this update, DELTA Plant does not intentionally set non-essential first-party analytics or advertising cookies through its own page code.

  • Basic browsing: the site can be opened without creating an account or accepting marketing cookies from DELTA Plant.
  • Contact form: the contact checkbox on the homepage is a data-processing consent for the message itself. It is not a separate advertising-cookie consent layer.
  • Optional audio: if visitors play the soundtrack, the audio file is requested from a third-party media host.
Important. Even when DELTA Plant does not intentionally set its own non-essential cookies, infrastructure or third-party providers may still use their own technical identifiers under their own policies.
Section 3

Third-party technologies and possible identifiers

Category Provider Why it is used Notes
Hosting / delivery GitHub Pages / GitHub infrastructure Serves the public static website May involve provider-managed logs, cache identifiers, or security-related session handling outside DELTA Plant's direct control.
Fonts Google Fonts Delivers the Manrope and Space Grotesk typefaces Browser requests to font services may involve technical identifiers needed by the provider.

Contact relay Web3Forms Transmits submitted contact requests securely Activated only when the visitor sends the form. The relay may process submission metadata and its own anti-abuse signals.
Optional media Pixabay audio hosting Streams the optional soundtrack when enabled by the visitor No audio request is made until the visitor interacts with playback controls.
External destinations Telegram, GitHub, and other linked platforms Open external project channels or documents Once a visitor leaves deltaplant.ai, the destination service applies its own cookie and privacy policy.
Section 4

Managing preferences

Visitors can manage cookies and similar identifiers through browser settings. Common options include blocking third-party cookies, clearing stored site data, using private browsing mode, or disabling media autoplay and external content permissions.

  • You can browse the core landing page without submitting the form.
  • You can avoid optional media requests by leaving the soundtrack muted.
  • You can decline to open Telegram, GitHub, or other third-party destinations if you do not want those platforms to receive a visit.
Section 5

Updates and contact

If the DELTA Plant website introduces new embedded tools, analytics, or consent flows, this page should be updated to reflect that change. The latest revision date appears at the top of the policy.

Questions related to cookies or similar identifiers can be sent through the homepage contact form. For broader privacy information, see the Privacy Policy.
